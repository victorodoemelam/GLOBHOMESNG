Inside Globhomes · Residential Interior

Bold Tiles Interior

A confident interior composition where tactile surfaces and bespoke furniture take centre stage.

Project typeResidential Interior
LocationNigeria
ScopeInterior composition & furniture

The design story.

The visual language embraces contrast and personality. Bold material choices are balanced by restrained furniture forms, allowing each surface to make an impact while the room remains harmonious and easy to inhabit.

Globhomes approached the project as a complete composition, considering proportion, movement, materials and the relationship between every custom-made element.

“Bold choices feel timeless when every detail has a reason.”

The result reflects our belief that excellent interiors should elevate the everyday: beautiful to look at, natural to use and made to hold their value over time.
