GrenHouse Abuja

A considered interior scheme balancing clean architectural lines with comfort, warmth and character.

Project typeFurniture & Interior
LocationAbuja
ScopeFurniture, finishes & styling

The design story.

For GrenHouse Abuja, the aim was to make every space feel polished but welcoming. Bespoke furniture anchors each room, while layered textures and carefully selected finishes soften the contemporary geometry.

Globhomes approached the project as a complete composition, considering proportion, movement, materials and the relationship between every custom-made element.

“Strong design is not only seen. It is felt in the way every element belongs.”

The result reflects our belief that excellent interiors should elevate the everyday: beautiful to look at, natural to use and made to hold their value over time.
