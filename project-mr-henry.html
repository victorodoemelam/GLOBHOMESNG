Mr Henry Residence

A warm, composed home where bespoke furniture and tactile finishes create an effortless sense of comfort.

Project typeResidential Interior
LocationNigeria
ScopeInterior styling & bespoke furniture

The design story.

The design direction centres on quiet confidence: clean silhouettes, rich material contrasts and pieces that feel substantial without overwhelming the room. Every element was considered as part of one calm, cohesive experience.

Globhomes approached the project as a complete composition, considering proportion, movement, materials and the relationship between every custom-made element.

“A home should feel collected over time, yet completely resolved from the first day.”

The result reflects our belief that excellent interiors should elevate the everyday: beautiful to look at, natural to use and made to hold their value over time.
